Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to provide?

For the External Security Checkup, just your website URL — we handle everything else. For the Business Assessment, we'll need temporary admin access to your cloud workspace (M365 or Google), which we'll walk you through on the kickoff call.

Will this disrupt my business?

No. Our testing is non-intrusive and designed to have zero impact on your operations. We never perform denial-of-service testing or anything that could take your systems offline.

What happens after I pay?

You'll receive a confirmation email within 1 hour. We'll schedule a 15-minute kickoff call to confirm scope, then begin testing. Your report is delivered on schedule with a debrief call to walk through everything.

Who fixes the issues you find?

Your report includes step-by-step remediation instructions that your IT person or provider can follow. If you don't have IT support, we can help fix issues for an additional fee, or recommend a trusted provider.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We sign a confidentiality agreement before every engagement. All findings are encrypted and shared only with you. We carry professional liability insurance and follow strict data handling protocols.

Why is the pricing so low right now?

We're offering launch pricing to our first clients. These rates will increase to standard pricing soon. Lock in the current rate by booking now.
