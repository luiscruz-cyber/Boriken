## Methodology

Our assessment follows industry-standard frameworks:
- **OWASP Testing Guide v4.2** — Web application security
- **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)** — Overall security posture
- **PTES** — Penetration Testing Execution Standard
- **CIS Controls v8** — Prioritized security actions

### Phases
1. **Kickoff & Scoping** — Understand your environment and objectives
2. **Reconnaissance** — Map your external attack surface
3. **Assessment** — Test for vulnerabilities and misconfigurations
4. **Analysis** — Validate findings and assess business impact
5. **Reporting** — Deliver findings with actionable remediation steps
6. **Debrief** — Walk through findings with your team
